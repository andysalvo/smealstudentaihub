### Mobile-First is Non-Negotiable

49% of students say a positive mobile experience improves their view of an institution. Gen Z uses mobile as primary research tool. Design mobile-first, then enhance for desktop.

### Audience Segmentation in Navigation

University of Arizona uses an "I am..." dropdown routing prospective students, faculty, and parents to tailored content. For Student AI Hub, potential segments:
- Club members (events, resources, labs)
- Prospective members (about, join)
- Faculty advisors (governance, mission)
- External visitors (resources, about)

### Clear Information Architecture

- Limit main menu to 5-7 items max
- Group by content type, not organizational hierarchy
- Every page reachable in 3 clicks or fewer
- Shallow depth, not deep nesting

### Strategic White Space and Visual Hierarchy

Academic sites tend to overcrowd. Use:
- Generous spacing
- Clear heading hierarchy
- Let content breathe
- Exemplars: Rhode Island School of Design, Adelphi University

### Interactive Elements That Add Value

Not decoration:
- UW-Madison: interactive timeline for history content
- Loyola Maryland: hover states revealing additional context
- Rice University: 360-degree campus exploration

### AI Transparency

Stanford features "Generative AI Policy Guidelines" prominently. For a Student AI Hub, being explicit about how AI is discussed and used builds credibility. This is our differentiator.

### Fresh Content Signals Life

A blog, event calendar, or news section that updates regularly prevents the site from looking abandoned. Even a "last updated" timestamp on resource pages shows the site is maintained.

---

## Recommendations for Student AI Hub

1. **Hero section** with one clear CTA (join the club / explore resources)
2. **Resource library** with search/filter (not just a list of links)
3. **Events section** with calendar integration
4. **Member/leadership showcase**
5. **Footer** with Penn State branding, accessibility statement, social links
6. **AI transparency page** -- how AI was used to build and maintain the site
7. **Fresh content signal** -- events feed or "recently updated" section
