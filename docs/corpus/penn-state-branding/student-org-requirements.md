# Penn State Student Organization Requirements

> Last updated: 2026-03-24
> Category: penn-state-branding
> Confidence: Official (Penn State policies AD07, AD54, AD69, Student Affairs)

---

## Naming Rules (Mandatory)

**Source:** [Student Affairs - Brand & Logo Guidelines](https://studentaffairs.psu.edu/involvement-student-life/student-services/hub-marketing-office/brand-logo-guidelines-student-orgs)
**Source:** [Policy AD07](https://policy.psu.edu/policies/ad07)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

- **CORRECT:** "Applied AI at Penn State" (org name + "at Penn State")
- **INCORRECT:** "Penn State Applied AI" ("Penn State" cannot come first unless name includes Club/Society/Association/Chapter)
- On licensed products, use only the registered organization name

## Trademark and Logo Rules (Mandatory)

**Source:** [Student Affairs - Brand & Logo Guidelines](https://studentaffairs.psu.edu/involvement-student-life/student-services/hub-marketing-office/brand-logo-guidelines-student-orgs)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

### What Student Orgs CANNOT Use

- Penn State University Shield / Lion Shield
- Penn State Intercollegiate Athletics logo
- Block "S" logo
- Nittany Lion mascot or Nittany Lion Shrine imagery
- The University Seal
- Penn State building images (even hand-drawn)
- "Penn State" text within a custom logo
- Any recreated or modified trademarked university graphics

### What Student Orgs CAN Use (with rules)

- **Paw print graphic** -- trademarked, unaltered, registration symbol on merch
- **University-Recognized Student Organization Shield Mark** -- must be requested via online form, used unaltered
- **Lion Shrine images** -- approved variety only, cannot be redrawn

### Merchandise

All merch with Penn State marks must use licensed vendors only. Contact: licensing@psu.edu

## Web Standards (Student Orgs Exempt)

**Source:** [Policy AD54](https://policy.psu.edu/policies/ad54)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

Policy AD54 (Web Page Design and Image) **explicitly does not apply to** student organizations. You are not required to follow the standard Penn State web template.

However, voluntarily following the brand color palette and typography gives the site legitimacy without running afoul of trademark rules.

---

## Accessibility (Student Orgs Exempt, But Recommended)

**Source:** [Policy AD69](https://policy.psu.edu/policies/ad69)
**Source:** [Penn State Accessibility Checklist](https://accessibility.psu.edu/checklist/)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

AD69 **does not legally bind RSO websites.** Student pages that "do not conduct University related business" are outside the policy scope.

**We're building to WCAG 2.2 AA anyway** because:

- It's the right thing to do for inclusivity
- It protects against accessibility complaints
- It makes us Smeal-ready if the site ever becomes semi-official
- Our FFC infrastructure already targets 90% Lighthouse accessibility

## Domain and Hosting

**Source:** [Student Affairs - RSO Policies](https://studentaffairs.psu.edu/student-life/student-orgs/manage-org/policies)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

- **sites.psu.edu** -- WordPress hosting, free for recognized RSOs
- **Only PSU web space** can be listed in the official Student Organization Directory
- RSOs can maintain external sites but they won't be linked from the directory
- **Recommended approach:** Maintain both a sites.psu.edu page (for directory listing) AND our GitHub Pages site

---

## Discover (OrgCentral) Requirements (Mandatory)

**Source:** [Discover Penn State](https://discover.psu.edu/)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

- All recognized orgs must maintain their Discover profile
- Officer and Advisor information must be kept current
- Membership roster (minimum 10 active members) maintained
- Constitution uploaded and approved
- **Failure to update = administrative suspension of all organizational privileges**

---

## Disclaimer (Recommended)

No mandatory disclaimer language exists for RSO websites. However, AD07 prohibits creating "ambiguity or confusion about University endorsement." A brief disclaimer is smart practice:

> "Applied AI at Penn State is a registered student organization. Views expressed do not represent Penn State University."

---

## Privacy and Data (Mandatory on PSU-Hosted Sites)

**Source:** [Policy AD53](https://policy.psu.edu/policies/ad53)
**Accessed:** 2026-03-24 | **Confidence:** Official

- Cannot store unencrypted PII on sites.psu.edu
- Email addresses for private communication only, not distributed to third parties
- If collecting emails on our external site, include a privacy notice
