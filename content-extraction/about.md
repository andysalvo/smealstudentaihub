## About the Student AI Hub

The Student AI Hub is a student-led initiative dedicated to building meaningful AI fluency at Penn State.

The Hub provides structured, source-based material designed to help students understand how artificial intelligence systems function, how they are deployed in business and education, and how to apply them responsibly in academic and professional settings.

The Penn State Smeal College of Business prepares students to make informed decisions and act with integrity. It emphasizes analytical thinking, ethical judgment, and understanding real-world business problems.

Students entering the workforce will be expected not only to use AI tools, but to evaluate their outputs, understand their limitations, and operate within clear ethical and professional standards.

The Hub exists to support that preparation.

Applied AI at Penn State maintains this site.

For more information, visit [appliedaipsu.com](http://appliedaipsu.com).
