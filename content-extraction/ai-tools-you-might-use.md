# AI Tools You Might Use | Student AI Hub

## Helpful AI Tools

This page is presented as a demonstration of how content in the Learn section will be structured as the Student AI Hub continues to develop.

When complete, this section will outline categories of AI tools students may encounter, along with high-level explanations of their intended use and constraints. The focus will be on understanding capabilities and tradeoffs, not on directing usage. The [AI Basics](https://ajs10845.wixsite.com/student-ai-hub-1/ai-basics) page serves as a full reference for how Learn pages will be developed and reviewed.

## Anthropic Certificates

Anthropic AI Courses are free learning resources from Anthropic that help students use Claude for studying, writing, research, and career planning in a more responsible way. Instead of only showing what Claude can do, these courses teach better prompting, stronger critical thinking, and when students still need to make their own judgment. That makes them a useful next step for students who want to use Claude more effectively in class and future professional work.
